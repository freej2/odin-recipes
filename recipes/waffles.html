Waffles

Yummy Waffle

Ingredients

  • Waffle Mix
  • Water

Steps

  1. Combine waffle mix and water in bowl and stir to smooth mixture
  2. With waffle maker heated up and sprayed, pour in waffle mix
  3. After cooking for 2 minutes 30s, remove waffle
  4. Put butter and syrup on waffle
  5. Eat it up
